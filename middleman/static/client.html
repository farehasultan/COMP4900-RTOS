Formula 1 Engine Simulator

  • Here you will see live updates of the F1 Engine's RPM. The throttle setting will influence its speed.
  • Enter a throttle setting between 0.00 and 1.00 into the textbox to change the engine's throttle setting.
  • 0.00 represents minimum throttle, 1.00 represents maximum throttle.
  • If you enter a number outside of this range, it will be adjusted to be within this range.
  • The engine requires a throttle of ~0.33 or higher for it to maintain speed.
  • Throttles below 0.33 will deaccelerate the engine, while above will accelerate the engine.
  • The engine will override your throttle if it is below 1500 RPM (if it's less than this value: 0.01 per 1RPM below) to achieve idle RPM. This usually is 1467 RPM.
  • The engine will hard-cut your throttle to 0 if the RPM is above 12000. Use throttle values like 0.334 to observe this better.
